63/* The legal range of a DCT coefficient is
64 * -1024 .. +1023 for 8-bit data;
65 * -16384 .. +16383 for 12-bit data.
66 * Hence the magnitude should always fit in 10 or 14 bits respectively.
67 */

71#else
